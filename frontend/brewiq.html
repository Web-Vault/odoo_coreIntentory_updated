How it works

Up and running in 4 steps.

1
Connect Your Branches

Add your café locations, set capacity, assign managers and configure supplier contacts.

2
Import Ingredients

Upload your ingredient catalog with units, reorder levels, and expiry rules. BrewIQ learns your menu structure.

3
AI Learns Your Patterns

After 7 days, the AI model calibrates demand forecasts using your sales data, footfall, and seasonal patterns.

4
Sit Back & Brew

BrewIQ handles reorders, waste alerts, and transfers automatically. You focus on the coffee.
